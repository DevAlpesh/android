Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Fantasy Girl: Anime Wallpaper, developed and operated by Alpesh Raval (Developer). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

About Our App

Fantasy Girl: Anime Wallpaper is a wallpaper application that provides high-quality anime wallpapers for your device. Our commitment is to provide this service while respecting and protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not collect any personal information directly from users.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect the following information:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Storage permissions (only when setting wallpapers)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the wallpaper service
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Set wallpapers on your device when requested
  • Debug and optimize app functionality

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information processed through our app:

  • Wallpaper images are temporarily cached on your device for better performance
  • We do not store any personal user data on external servers
  • All app interactions are processed locally on your device
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for downloading wallpapers
  • Cached images are stored securely in your device's private storage area

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Cached wallpaper images are temporarily stored on your device and are automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • We do not retain any personal data on our servers as all processing is done locally
  • You can clear the app's cache at any time through your device's settings

Your Data Rights

Since our app processes all data locally on your device, you maintain full control over your data. You can:

  • Clear cached images through your device's settings
  • Revoke permissions through your device's settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Permissions Required

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage permission: Required to set wallpapers on your device
  • Internet access: Required to download wallpaper images

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services
  • Android's wallpaper service

All third-party services are bound by their respective privacy policies and our data handling requirements.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes through:

  • App updates
  • In-app notifications
  • Updated privacy policy on our website

The date of the latest revision will always be listed at the top of the Privacy Policy.
