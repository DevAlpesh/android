Welcome to our Wallpaper App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect the following information:

We use the collected information to:

Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
