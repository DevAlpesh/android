Introduction

Welcome to IPO GMP Tracker, developed and operated by Alpesh Raval (Developer). About Our App

IPO GMP Tracker is a financial information application that provides real-time and historical data about upcoming IPOs, Grey Market Premium rates, subscription statistics, and financial analysis. Our commitment is to provide accurate financial information while respecting and protecting your privacy.

Disclaimer: This app provides information for educational and research purposes only. It should not be considered as financial advice. Please consult with qualified financial advisors before making investment decisions.

Our app operates under the following regulatory considerations:

  • GLBA Compliance: While we aggregate financial information, we do not collect sensitive financial data such as account numbers, SSNs, or payment information
  • Data Safety Requirements: We comply with Google Play Store's Data Safety requirements and declare all data collection practices transparently

Information You Provide

We do not collect any personal information directly from users. Our app operates without requiring:

  • User accounts or registration

  • Contact details or demographic information

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect the following information automatically:

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide real-time IPO information and GMP data
  • Send personalized notifications about IPO updates (based on locally stored preferences)
  • Maintain watchlist functionality locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience through anonymous usage analytics
  • Debug and optimize app functionality through anonymous crash reports
  • Generate aggregated, anonymous statistics for service improvement

Important: We do not use any personally identifiable information because we do not collect it. All personalization is based on locally stored device preferences.

Data Sources and Accuracy

Our app aggregates financial information from various public sources:

  • Stock exchanges and regulatory filing databases
  • Financial news websites and market data providers
  • Grey market premium tracking services
  • Official company announcements and prospectuses

While we strive for accuracy, financial markets are volatile and information may change rapidly. Users should verify information independently before making financial decisions.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Financial data is cached locally on your device for offline access
  • User preferences are stored securely in local device storage only
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • All user preferences and settings remain on your device
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments
  • Anonymous data transmission without personal identifiers

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Financial data cache is automatically updated and older data is removed periodically
  • User preferences and watchlists are retained until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized after 12 months
  • You can clear all app data at any time through your device's settings
  • Account data (if any) can be deleted upon request
